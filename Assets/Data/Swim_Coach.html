Week 1 - Workout 1

Distance: 1800m
For this workout you need: Pull Buoy, Kickboard

Warm Up - 400m
1 x 300m
Swim front crawl.
▸ Alternate 50m relaxed and 50m strong swim.

1 x 100m
Swim towfloat drill.

Main Set - 1100m
1 x 200m
Swim relaxed front crawl with a constant breathing pattern.
▸ Breathe every 5th stroke.

5 x 100m
Practice your flutter kick with a kickboard.
▸ Rest 20s after each interval

1 x 400m
Swim 300m easy breaststroke, then 100m fast.
▸ Try to swim relaxed during the first 300m of this exercise so that you can swim fast on the last 100m.

Time Trial - 100m
1 x 100m
Swim with sprint speed in this 100m time trial.
▸ You can log your time in the workout log.

Cool Down - 200m
2 x 100m
Swim this set with a stroke style of your choice.
▸ Mix in some drills.
▸ Rest 30s after each interval

Week 1 - Workout 4

Distance: 1500m

Warm Up - 400m
2 x 200m
Choose your favorite drill for this exercise.
▸ Examples: fingertip drag, towfloat, superman
▸ Rest 30s after each interval

Main Set - 800m
2 x 100m
Swim backstroke.
▸ Increase effort level from 1 to 4 with every 25m. The last 25m should be your fastest!
▸ Rest 20s after each interval

2 x 200m
Swim relaxed front crawl with a changing breathing pattern.
▸ Breathe after 3, then after 5, and then after 7 strokes. Then breathe after 5 and finally after 3 strokes. Repeat until the end of the exercise.
▸ Rest 30s after each interval

1 x 200m
Swim a reverse medley sequence with 50m each (front crawl, breaststroke, backstroke, butterfly).

Cool Down - 300m
1 x 300m
Swim easy back stroke.
